The Beginning of Aarav’s Journey

Aarav lived in a small village surrounded by green fields and tall trees.
He was curious, intelligent, and full of dreams, but life in the village was simple and sometimes limiting.
Every morning, he would walk to the small school under the shade of the old banyan tree,
thinking about the world beyond the village.

A Curious Mind

Aarav loved learning.
While other children played near the river or helped in the fields,
he spent his time reading old books, solving puzzles, and observing nature.
He asked questions — sometimes too many — and often surprised his teachers with his understanding.

Supportive Family

Aarav’s parents, Raghunath and Meera, encouraged his curiosity.
“Learn as much as you can, Aarav,” they would say.
“Knowledge will take you places, even beyond this village.”
Though their resources were limited, their love and guidance were endless.

The First Dream

One evening, Aarav sat under the banyan tree, looking at the sunset.
He thought, “I want to do something great. I want to make my village proud and help others too.”
That was the moment Aarav set his first dream —
to excel in studies and show that even a child from a small village could shine.

Chapter 1’s End

The stars twinkled in the sky as Aarav closed his notebook.
He felt a spark of hope and determination.
Little did he know, this was only the beginning of a long journey filled with challenges, learning, and dreams waiting to be fulfilled.
